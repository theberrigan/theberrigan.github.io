FAQ

What is neoRide Postpaid?
neoRide postpaid offers a secure and convenient mobile payment solution for toll road users. Pay tolls seamlessly using Google Pay, Apple Pay, or credit/debit card.
How does it work?
neoRide postpaid streamlines toll payments: Register your vehicle, drive on toll roads, and receive invoices via SMS for secure approval and payment. Enroll in automatic payments for added convenience.
How much do tolls cost with neoRide Postpaid?
The toll charge reflects the posted rate for the route you travel on the toll road. An additional 15% surcharge is applied to cover the cost of processing the transaction on your behalf.
How long does it take to get a bill to be sent to me?
After you drive on toll roads, it may take anywhere from 2 days to 4 weeks for us to receive your toll invoice from the tolling authority. Once we do, we’ll send you a text notification letting you know your toll bill is ready to pay. Don’t worry — you’ll still have at least 48 hours to make the payment after invoice is generated.
My neoRide Postpaid account was deactivated before I could approve my toll payment.
Toll payment approval is required within the time specified in the text from the first invoice you receive. If you would like to be reinstated to use neoRide Postpaid in the future, please go to Contact Us and send us a message.
Will neoRide Postpaid work on any cell phone?
neoRide Postpaid is compatible with smartphones and tablet devices, requiring only internet access and text messaging capabilities.
What is the advantage of using neoRide Postpaid?
neoRide Postpaid makes toll payments easy and stress-free. You’ll receive clear invoices, timely reminders, and the option for automatic payments for added convenience. Choose from multiple payment methods, including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and credit or debit cards. While your vehicle is active with us, you won’t have to worry about fines or extra fees from tolling authorities.
Will neoRide Postpaid work on any Toll roads nationwide?
neoRide Postpaid covers 95% of toll fascilities in the US. To see our list of coverage, please visit www.neoride.com/coverage.
Do I need to have a local license plate to drive on toll roads?
No. It will work with any U.S. state license plate.
Is this a type of toll tag?
No, neoRide Postpaid is a payment service that does not require a toll tag to pay for your tolls. We recommend using our neoRide app if you would like to use a toll tag.
What if I have a toll tag already?
To ensure a smooth transition, please cancel your current toll tag account and remove the tag from your vehicle before registering with neoRide Postpaid.
Do I have to register/sign up?
Yes, our registration process is simple and can be completed in just a few minutes.
How do I register or signup?
Create an account on postpaid.neoride.com by entering the cell phone number and follow a wizard style sign up process.
What does it cost to register/signup?
Get a full year of stress-free travel with neoRide for just $4.99 per vehicle! Your membership renews automatically each year, so you’ll never have to worry about lapses in coverage. Enjoy the road with confidence—no toll authority fees, no hassle, just the easiest way to manage tolls.
What happens if I sell my car or buy a new one?
If you sell your existing vehicle and do not transfer the license plate from that vehicle to your new vehicle you can login into your account at postpaid.neoride.com to easily manage your vehicles.
I have toll violation notices. Can I pay those with neoRide Postpaid?
Transactions that occurred prior to signing up for tapNpay must be settled with your current account with the toll agency. However, any tolls after registering with tapNpay will be paid with the service.
Will I still get toll invoices in the mail?
You may still get invoices from the toll agency for any balances still owed for tolls prior to the start of your neoRide Postpaid account. You will get e-invoices from neoRide Postpaid while you are participating in the system, provided you approve your e-invoices within the allotted timeframe.
We drive multiple vehicles. Can I have all those vehicles on the same account?
Yes, all vehicles registered using the same cell phone number will be on the same neoRide Postpaid account based on the membership model you selected. Enjoy the convenience of having up to 7 vehicles registered to your account.
Will it work if I drive a motorcycle?
neoRide Postpaid works for motorcycles as well.
Who do I contact for help with my account?
Have questions or concerns? Our support team is here to help! Visit postpaid.neoride.com and click Contact Us to reach out.
How much time do I have to pay tolls with neoRide Postpaid?
Payment approval is required within the specified time from the first invoice you receive. Need more time? Reach out to our customer support team to see how we can help.
What if I already have another toll payment service?
If you are currently using another fleet service, you will need to remove your license plate from that account before registering with neoRide Postpaid to ensure a seamless registration process.
How can I find previously approved toll bills in tapNpay?
Go to postpaid.neoride.com, enter your phone number and the pin you will receive via text. Once logged in, you can navigate to your current pending invoices or see your paid invoice history.
Can I make changes to my toll authority tag through neoRide Postpaid?
neoRide Postpaid is a toll payment facilitator, making it easy to pay your untagged tolls using a variety of payment methods. neoRide Postpaid is not associated with the tolling authority and cannot answer any questions associated with you current toll tag.
Can I change my payment method?
Yes, simply login to your account at postpaid.neoride.com, go to Profile then tap "Change Payment Method."
My account was deactivated before I could approve my toll payment.
Toll payment approval is required within the designated time from the first invoice you receive. If you would like to be reinstated to use neoRide Postpaid in the future, please go to Contact Us and send us a message.
How can I add a license plate to my account?
To register a new license plate, visit postpaid.neoride.com and follow the online prompts. Adding the new plate online will allow you to pay for tolls for both the new vehicle and the original vehicle on your account.
How can I remove a license plate from my account?
Go to postpaid.neoride.com, enter your phone number and the pin you will receive via text. Once logged in, navigate to the "My Account" page in the menu. There you can deregister the license plate from your account.
